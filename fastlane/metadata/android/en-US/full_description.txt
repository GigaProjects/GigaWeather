GigaWeather - A Modern Weather App for Android

GigaWeather helps you check current conditions and forecasts for the places you care about.

KEY FEATURES:

MULTIPLE CITIES
- Add and manage saved locations
- Quickly switch between cities
- Mark a default location

FORECASTS
- Detailed daily forecast
- Hourly weather details
- Temperature, wind, humidity, sunrise, and sunset data

CURRENT LOCATION
- Use your phone location when you want nearby weather
- Search by city name or coordinates

CUSTOM SETTINGS
- Celsius or Fahrenheit
- km/h or mph wind speeds
- Light, dark, and Material You dynamic colors

WIDGETS AND NOTIFICATIONS
- Home screen weather widget
- Weather notifications and change alerts

GigaWeather runs directly on your phone and uses public weather endpoints by default. No custom domain or account is required.
